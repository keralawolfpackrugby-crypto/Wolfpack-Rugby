KERALA WOLFPACK RUGBY CLUB

Rise Together As One Pack

Our Philosophy

We believe that rugby is more than a game—it is a way of life. Every player is trained to think, adapt, support one another, and rise together as one pack. Discipline, humility, and hard work form the foundation of everything we do.

Our Vision

To create a strong rugby culture in Kerala that nurtures talent, promotes equality, and provides young athletes with opportunities to grow through sport, education, and life skills.

Our Mission

  • To identify, train, and develop rugby talent across Kerala
  • To provide structured pathways from grassroots to competitive rugby
  • To empower youth, especially from coastal communities, through sport-based education and discipline
  • To promote values such as teamwork, respect, leadership, and perseverance

What We Do

  • Grassroots rugby development programs
  • Competitive training for sevens and fifteens rugby
  • Physical conditioning, injury prevention, and performance-based training
  • Talent identification and long-term athlete development
  • Community engagement and outreach programs

Community & Impact

Kerala Wolfpack strongly believes in giving back to the community. Along with our broader mission across Kerala, we place special focus on promoting and guiding youth from the coastal regions of Malabar in Kerala.

We actively encourage and support young players from coastal and underrepresented backgrounds, helping them access better opportunities in sports, education, and personal growth. Rugby becomes a tool not just for competition, but for building character, confidence, and life direction.

About Us

Kerala Wolfpack Rugby Club is built on strength, unity, and opportunity. Founded with a deep passion for the game and a strong sense of purpose, our club is committed to developing rugby across Kerala while shaping confident, disciplined, and resilient individuals on and off the field.
